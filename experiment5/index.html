Description

In this assignment, we built a generator which takes in three images that we have chosen, reads them, and creates a rendered, parameterized image.

Technical

You can switch between the three images using the drop down, and we have a slider that goes from 0-100% which controls the mutation rate. Then, we show a picture of the inspired picture being changed, along with an active score and exploration rate. After that, the best picture is changed, but this picture also changes as time goes on and the generator finds a better match. Then, we move on to showing the best score and best images that come after the best one.

Reflection

It was fun getting to choose what pictures to do, along with what shapes to draw them with. We enjoyed creating each image with a different shape, but also working with different colors and types of images.
